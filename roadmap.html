WHY EACH AGENCY MAKES THE NEXT SALE EASIER

It is an operating system, not a dashboard, and the pricing benchmark the category does not have. More agencies and creators running through SPCTR sharpens all three core elements.

  1. 1.Agency signs. The whole roster comes onto the platform.
  2. 2.Creators authorize cross-platform analytics. Real performance data flows in.
  3. 3.SPCTRscore recalibrates against the live market.
  4. 4.The Pricing Engine values gaming creator deals on game, niche, persona, and risk, computed from the authorized roster data.
  5. 5.The Coach learns from a sharper dataset, for both sides.
  6. 6.Sharper SPCTRscore, sharper Pricing Engine, sharper Coach. The next agency benefits more, willingness to pay rises, and the pricing benchmark becomes the number the category stands on.
